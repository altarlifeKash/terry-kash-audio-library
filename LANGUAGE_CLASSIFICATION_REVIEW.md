## Classification policy

- The website trusts the owner-maintained `language` value in `data/library.json`.
- `Armenian` is the normalized website value unless a source explicitly distinguishes Western or Eastern Armenian.
- Romanized titles do not override the owner’s classification of the recording language.
- Bandcamp metadata may assist review but must not replace an owner-confirmed value.
- Musical style, artwork, and personal names are not used as language evidence.
